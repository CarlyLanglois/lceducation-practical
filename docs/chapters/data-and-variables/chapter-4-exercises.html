4.6.3. Calculate a space mission!¶

We need to determine how many days it will take to reach Mars.

  1. Create and assign a Miles to Mars variable
    • Miles per kilometer * Distance to Mars
  2. Next, we need a variable to hold the hours it would take to get to Mars
    • Miles to Mars / Shuttle Speed
  3. Finally, declare a variable and assign it the value of days to Mars
    • Hours To Mars / 24
